About Me

Mai'Lin has 10 years of Therapy work learning Shiatsu from her Uncle and a mixed Modality her father developed which incorporated Spinal Adjustment, Reflexology elements and Shiatsu. Further expanded her knowledge studying Fascia work with Marius Strydom (who brought Rolfing to South Africa) as well as training workshops with Anatomy Training Structural Alignement, The Franklin Method and Anatomy in Motion. She is also a Bach Flowers Practioner and a Quantum Touch Practitoner. Now adding to her portfolio is Spinal Flow, a very gentle, yet profound modality that relates well to Mai'Lin. She brings her knowledge and experience, including working with Hospice in South Africa and as a Carer in the UK. HoldING a MSc from the Centre for Alternative Technology, Wales and UEL.
